How to Stay Safe

We all know wildfires are very dangerous. You might be scared that you will get burned. You could possibly die. But you may not. How? What should you do in case a wildfire starts? There are many questions on how to stay safe if one happens. Here are some things that you will need to know in case a wildfire starts:

  • Wear masks in fire.
  • Have a positive mindset.
  • Get help immediately.
  • Stay away from the smoke.
  • Evacuate ASAP.
  • Tuck, duck, and roll.

You don't want smoke to get in your lungs. It is really important that you don't inhale the smoke. You want to think that you will get out of there alive. It is important to have a positive mindset. When caught in flames, you want to tuck, duck, and roll. This will get the flames off you. These tips will help you succesfully stay safe from a wildfire.
