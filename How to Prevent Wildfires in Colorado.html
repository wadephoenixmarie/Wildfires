How to Prevent Wildfires in Colorado

There are many ways we can prevent wildfires. Some ways are:

  • Don't light a fire in dry areas.
  • Light a fire in a camping pit, or you can make one.
  • Wildfires can start from lightning, lit cigarette butts, and more.
  • If you are camping, do not light anything on fire unless it is in a campfire pit.
  • If you smoke, it is best to move away from trees as much as possible.
  • When you are camping, make sure to put your fire all the way out.

This deadly disaster can be prevented from these steps above. Colorado is a really dry state, so it causes more wildfires. You can't start a campfire in really dry areas, or it might start a wildfire. If you want to start a campfire, these things below will improve the amount of water in the grass.

  • Water dry areas twice a weak.
  • Before you start a campfire, it is best to water the vegetation around it.
  • Keep a bucket of water nearby.
  • Clear all of the vegetation from the campfire before you leave the campsite.

There are more things that you can do in order to prevent a wildfire. If you click on the "Who to Contact" tab, there are resources you can contact to prevent a wildfire. If you do these things, there will be less of a chance for a wildfire to start. The small things help a lot.
