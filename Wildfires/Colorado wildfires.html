Colorado Wildfires

There is a wildfire in Durango, Colorado that has made over 300 households evacuate and has closed 1 highway. The wildfire is named Fire 416. It has burned over 1,000 acres.

The Hayman Fire is the largest wildfire in Colorado history. Due to a dry climate, we have lots of wildfires. 674.375 sq. miles were burned due to wildfires in the summer of 2018. Colorado has lots of droughts, so there are many wildfires.

Although these wild beasts are dangerous and threatening, they can be tamed. We just have to learn how to do it the right way without hurting the wild animals or vegetation.
